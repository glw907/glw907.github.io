30-Day Digital Declutter

25 Feb 2019, 22:54

I’m just about to embark on the “Digital Declutter” exercise detailed in Digital Minimalism: Choosing a Focused Life in a Noisy World by Cal Newport. The process includes three steps: Step one is putting aside a thirty-day period during which I will take a break from all optional technologies. That starts today! Step two is exploring and reengaging in non-digital activities and behaviors that I find particularly satisfying and meaningful. I’m honestly pretty excited about this part of the exercise.
